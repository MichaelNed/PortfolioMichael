CUSTOMER WEBSITE
FOR A BEAUTY SALON

An informative website realised for a Dutch beauty salon, including branding, design, and search engine optimisation. Dutch is the language used on the website because it is situated in the Netherlands.

Brand Identity

A distinctive logo and design help your business stand out. I created a comprehensive business design bundle to achieve this. This includes graphics like posters, brochures, and logos. All of this gives the brand its own identity. You can see some examples in the graphic below.

Design & Text
The design is created in close collaboration with the business. The goal was to create concise paragraphs of catchy text to make the information straightforward. The business wished to retain the original design on the homepage. So, the pinkish colors with the white rose are used as a base. And, a three-colored spectrum is used to provide a comprehensive design.
Optimazation
I used meta specifications plus page quality checks to optimize the website for search engines (SEO). I also focused on enhancing my Google ranking by adopting a better heading and link structure. This process also included adding alternative text to all images on the website. Lastly, keywords and several backlinks were generated to boost the SEO score even more.
Coding
The website is fully personalized using HTML & CSS. You can use this website on any screen you prefer, your phone, laptop, or tablet. This website is also linked to Google Analytics in order to evaluate the activity of visitors. Customers are staying on the website for a relatively long time, and the website is considered quite active for this branch.

Design Choice

In order to achieve the best fit, the style was developed in close partnership with the business. The Business wanted a streamlined and modern look, where the customer knows where to find the needed information at a glance.
